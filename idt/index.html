COMMITTEES DESCRIPTION

Our Student activity consists of more than 10 committees. Each is specialized in a certin field, with regular sessions every week.

IT Committee

This committee focus on web design. IT Members are responsible for:

  • website designing and development in both front end and back end.
  • Providing data base for each event.
  • developing registration systems and members’ online profiles.
  • registration in booth.
FR Committee

Members are IDT representatives in front of sponsors and they’re responsible for:

  • providing needed fund by conducting deals with sponsors.
  • maintaining good relations with current sponsors and create new relations.
  • establishing contacts database.
MEDIA Committee

Media Members are designers That create visual concepts by using computer in addition to photographing IDT events. They’re responsible for:

  • designing all printouts (banners, roll ups, flyers, t-shirts & IDs).
  • implementing all needed designs and videos for social media channels.
  • covering all IDT events through photography.
HR Recruitment Committee

Human resources recruiters develop and implement recruiting plans and strategies designed to fulfill company staffing needs. HR-Recruitment Member are responsible for:

  • Designing and implementing the overall recruiting strategy.
  • Set selection phases and criteria for each event.
  • Apply coordination system for Institute workshops.
HR OD Committee

Organizational development (OD) is the process through which an organization improves its internal capacity to meet its current and possible future requirements. HR-OD Members are mainly in contact with internal members in IDT. And They’re responsible for:

  • Applying coordination system in all committees.
  • Feedback interviews for internal IDT members.
  • Developing members’ evaluation, motivation & feedback system.
PR Committee

Public relation and digital marketing specialists build and maintain a positive image for a organization and maintain a marketing campaigns for the social media. PR Responsible for:

  • Booth ushering.
  • Hosting all IDT events.
  • Managing IDT social Media Platforms throughout the year.
  • Maintaining good relations with IDT media Sponsors.
  • Building good relations with the college administration and other student activities.
Logistics Committee

Logistics and Reception Members are The main actors of The Offline Marketing in IDT. They’re responsible for:

  • designing and building booths.
  • Decorations in all events.
  • Purchasing needed material for IDT.
  • Reception all of IDT events.
AC Committee

Members in this committee are the main actors of INSTITUTE event which provides participants this year with the following workshops:

Electrical package:
  • Advanced programming.
  • Embedded software & hardware.
  • Automation
    • Mechanical package:
  • Mechanical design software.
  • Industrial Engineering.
    • Managerial Package:
  • Marketing.
  • Project management.
